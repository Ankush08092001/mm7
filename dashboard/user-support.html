Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I upgrade my subscription plan?

To upgrade your subscription, go to the "Subscription" page in your dashboard. You'll see your current plan and available upgrade options. Click on the "Upgrade" button next to your desired plan, and follow the payment instructions. Your account will be upgraded immediately after successful payment, and you'll have access to all the new features.

Can I download course materials for offline use?

Yes, Pro and Premium plan subscribers can download course materials for offline use. When viewing a course, look for the download icon next to downloadable resources. Note that video content may have streaming-only restrictions due to copyright. Downloaded materials are for personal use only and should not be shared or distributed.

How do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Certificates are automatically generated when you complete all required modules and pass the final assessment with a score of at least 70%. You can access your certificates from the "Certificates" section in your dashboard. From there, you can view, download, or share your certificates. If you've completed a course but don't see your certificate, please contact support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions. For users in India, we also support UPI payments. All transactions are processed securely through our payment gateway. If you're having trouble with payment, please contact our billing support team.
